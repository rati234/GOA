Famous Painters

Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci was an Italian polymath whose interests included art, science, engineering, and more. He is considered one of the most influential artists in history.

Painting name: Mona Lisa
Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter known for his emotional and expressive style. His works are now among the most popular and valuable in the world.

Painting name: The Starry Night
Artist: Vincent van Gogh

Pablo Picasso

Pablo Picasso was a Spanish painter and sculptor, one of the founders of Cubism. His career spanned many styles and periods.

Painting name: Guernica
Artist: Pablo Picasso
